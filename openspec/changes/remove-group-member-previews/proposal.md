## Why

The dashboard group cards and account-group summary header currently expose member email previews. That preview is not needed for the workflow and adds noise to the grouped overview.

## What Changes

- remove the member preview block from dashboard group cards
- remove the member snapshot block from the account-group summary header
- keep member details available only in the dedicated members section below

## Impact

- grouped overview cards become shorter and less noisy
- member identities remain accessible in the proper detail area
